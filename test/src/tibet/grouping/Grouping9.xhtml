Group Test #9




This test sets up multiple nested 'tibet:group' elements, all of which enclose a set of descendant elements. Note that the overall 'fooGroup' group has no 'focuswrap' attribute on it that causes the group calculation machinery to *not* 'wrap' when calculating 'next' and 'previous' groups.

Elements that are members of the 'gooGroup' group will have their borders turned black, elements that are members of the 'mooGroup' group will have their borders turned red and elements that are members of the 'booGroup' group will have their borders turned green.
