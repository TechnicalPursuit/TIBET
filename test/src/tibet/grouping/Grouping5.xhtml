Group Test #5




This test sets up 2 'tibet:group' elements, both that, although they enclose a set of descendant elements, also have an explicit query that will associate elements in the document with that group.

Elements that are members of the 'fooGroup' group will have their borders turned black, those that are members of the 'gooGroup' group will have their borders turned blue.
