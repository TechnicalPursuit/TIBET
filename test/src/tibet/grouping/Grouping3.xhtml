Group Test #3




This test sets up 3 'tibet:group' elements, one that encloses a set of descendent elements (thereby forming an implicit 'descendant element' query) and two others with an explicit query that will associate elements in the document with that group.

Elements that are members of the 'fooGroup' group will have their borders turned black, those that are members of the 'gooGroup' group will have their borders turned blue and those that are members of 'nestedGooGroup' group will have their borders turned red.
